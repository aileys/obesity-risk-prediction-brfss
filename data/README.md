**Dataset Name:** Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS)  
**Source:** Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)  
**URL:** https://www.cdc.gov/brfss/  
**Description:**  
The BRFSS dataset contains annual health-related survey data collected from U.S. adults.  
It includes information on:
- Health behaviors (physical activity, smoking, alcohol use)
- Chronic conditions (obesity, diabetes, hypertension)
- Mental and physical health status
- Demographics (age, gender, education, income)
